Bio

Sean C, professionally known as XiØN is a rapper, songwriter, and producer raised in the Flatbush neighborhood of Brooklyn, New York. Born to Caribbean parents, he grew up surrounded by culture, rhythm, and street scenarios that shaped his style. Music became both an outlet and a form of reflection, with Hip-Hop serving as the foundation. Since 2007, he has worked behind the scenes with other artists while quietly developing his own sound. His style is rooted in storytelling, emotional depth, and lyrical sharpness that reflects the city that raised him. Even as Hip-Hop shifted toward Trap and Drill, XiØN stayed focused on authenticity, often weaving his wordplay into the melodic textures of R&B. While his raw, introspective raps carry a gritty edge, his versatility lets him lean into vulnerability, exploring themes of love, intimacy, and desire with the same unfiltered honesty that defines his street narratives. Now based in Tampa, Florida, he continues to make music that blends soulful introspection with street perspective.
