Bio

XiØN is a Rapper, Songwriter & Producer from Brooklyn, New York, now based in Tampa, Florida. Raised in the Flatbush area of Brooklyn before its gentrification, he was shaped by the raw reality of street life. The grit of classic Hip-Hop, the soul of R&B, and the rhythm of Reggae within this environment influenced his musical style. Known for his raw lyricism and introspective storytelling, his music is rooted in real-life experiences & relatable topics, resonating with listeners who crave substance over superficiality.
